# Approved Sources for Career Page Generation

Used by `generate_next_steps.py` to guide source selection in the prompt.

## Universal (all occupation types)

**Government / official data:**
- U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) — OOH, OEWS, employment projections
- O*NET OnLine — occupation definitions, task data
- NBER working papers — labor economics research

**Think tanks / research:**
- WEF Future of Jobs Report
- McKinsey Global Institute (research reports, not consulting blog)
- MIT Technology Review (data-backed pieces only)
- Accenture Research

**Major newspapers (labor/tech topics, data-backed only):**
- NYT, WSJ

---

## Technology / Software

- Stack Overflow Developer Survey (annual)
- GitHub Octoverse / GitHub Blog (research posts only)
- CNCF Annual Survey
- Linux Foundation research
- IEEE Spectrum
- Google Cloud research, AWS reports, Anthropic, Microsoft Research

---

## Sales / Business Development

- Salesforce State of Sales Report (annual practitioner survey)
- HubSpot State of Sales (annual practitioner survey)
- LinkedIn State of Sales Report
- Sales Management Association
- Harvard Business Review (data-backed pieces only)

---

## Insurance / Financial Services

- LIMRA (Life Insurance Marketing and Research Association)
- National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC)
- Deloitte Insights — insurance and financial services AI research
- McKinsey Financial Services research

---

## Real Estate

- National Association of Realtors (NAR)
- Zillow Research
- CoStar Group research

---

## Healthcare / Nursing

- American Nurses Association (ANA)
- AHRQ (Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality)
- New England Journal of Medicine (NEJM) — AI in clinical settings
- JAMA Network

---

## Aviation / Transportation

- FAA (Federal Aviation Administration)
- IATA (International Air Transport Association)
- Transportation Research Board

---

## Avoid (all domains)

- Gartner, IDC, Forrester — paywalled, URL rot
- Forbes contributor network, The New Stack — low editorial bar
- Vendor blogs (e.g. Salesforce blog ≠ Salesforce State of Sales report)
- Career advice sites: Indeed editorial, LinkedIn Learning blog, Glassdoor editorial
- Opinion pieces without data
- Undated content
- Sources older than 2 years unless no newer equivalent exists
